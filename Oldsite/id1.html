Homeward Bound Rescue League is a non-profit, all Volunteer, no-kill animal rescue organization dedicated to giving animals a second chance at life. We rescue, foster, and find permanent happy and loving homes for our rescued friends.

The fact that we are a no-kill organization and that we take all breeds sets us apart from other rescue leagues and shelters.

We started our rescue organization in 2007 and are very proud to say that since that time we have placed over 1000 dogs and cats into safe and caring homes.  Homeward Bound Rescue League is an entirely volunteer organization that embodies the sense of community by advocating humane and ethical principles.

Homeward Bound Rescue League is funded soley by donations and fund raising events and does not receive any local, state or federal funding. We have a 501(c)(3) tax-exempt status. Please consult your tax advisor regarding contributions to non-profit organizations.
