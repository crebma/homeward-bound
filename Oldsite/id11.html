We always have an immediate need for pet supply donations. We care for over 500+ animals every year. Donations can be dropped off at the Canton, MI Pet Supplies Plus store. Our donation bin is located to left hand side of the front door. You can also drop off any supplies directly to us at the Pet Supplies Plus store on the 1st and 3rd Saturday of every month between noon and 4:00PM.
