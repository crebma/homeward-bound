Bowl-a-thon
Mark your calendars! HBRL's 2nd Annual Bowling Fundraiser is scheduled for Saturday, June 30th! Last year, two stellar volunteers surprised us with this event and raised over $3,500! This year, with all our special medical cases, we're hoping to double or triple that! And if bowling's not your thing, you can still come - there's a great bar/restaurant area at Plaza Lanes and we'll be hosting a 50/50 and LOTS of prize raffles throughout the night. Stay tuned for more details, but if you're interested in donating a prize for the raffle, please let us know (email us at HBRL.fosters@gmail.com). There's also a great way for local businesses to advertise at Plaza Lanes that will benefit HBRL! Last year someone graciously donated Taylor Swift tickets and Tigers tickets, so we'd LOVE to get some big ticket items like that again, but we're also building gift baskets with lots of goodies and/or gift cards. As we get more of them together we'll be teasing you all on facebook, so if you see something you've got to have, you can be sure to be there!
