Myrah's story!
This was Myrah, a pure malamute. She was an owner surrender and a very excited anxious dog. They had kept her chained to a dog house for the first 9 months of her life and you could tell they did some work with her but they just didn’t have the time for her high energy. We had her for about 2 months. We worked her with my sister’s husky for a while and put her through intense socialization at my house. When they adopted her, I went to their home and consulted with them a few times on how to handle her and they’ve been doing great ever since.

– Homeward Bound
