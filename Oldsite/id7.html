Maggs

Hi, my name is Maggs. My mother [Rumor] was rescued when she was pregnant. I and 12 other puppies were born in a Homeward Bound Rescue League Foster home. I am about a year old now and I have developed a condition called “Hip Dysplasia” along with severe pain. I am in desperate need of hip surgery to correct this condition. Without this surgery I will continue to be in severe pain and will lose the ability to walk.

Homeward Bound Rescue League is raising money for my surgery, could you please donate?





Homeward Bound Rescue League is a 501[C][3] Non-Profit, all Volunteer organization.  Please consult your tax advisor regarding contributions to non-profit organizations. All monies raised go directly to the care and well being of the animals.
